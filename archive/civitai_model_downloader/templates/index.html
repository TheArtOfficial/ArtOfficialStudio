CivitAI Model Downloader

Follow these steps to download models from CivitAI:

1. Create a CivitAI account:
  1. Go to civitai.com
  2. Click "Sign In" in the top right corner
  3. Choose your preferred sign-in method (Google, Discord, or email)
  4. Complete the registration process
2. Generate your API token:
  1. Go to your Account Settings
  2. Scroll down to the "API Keys" section
  3. Click "Generate New Key"
  4. Copy the generated API token
3. Get the model URL:
  1. Browse models on CivitAI
  2. Click on the model you want to download
  3. Right click the "Download" button and click "Copy link address"
  4. If the link address contains "?", delete the "?" and everything after it
